About Us

Hotel Aifa, Strategically Located in Labuan's Revitalized Business District, is Minutes From The Airport and Ferry Terminal. The Hotel Features 139 Well-Appointed Rooms, Including 122 Deluxe Rooms (6 Junior and Executive), With Stunning Views Of Either The Harbor or The City. Guests Can Enjoy Two Food and Beverage Outlets, a Lobby Lounge, and in-Room Dining Service. Comprehensive Facilities, Including a Business Center, are Available, and The Hotel is Dedicated To Providing Excellent Service For Both Business and Leisure Travelers.
