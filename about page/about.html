About Us

Strategically located in the revitalized business district of Labuan, hotel aifa is only minute from the airport and ferry terminal the hotel has 139 well appointed rooms made up of 122 deluxe rooms (6 junior and executive). the rooms boast stunning views of either the harbor or the city of Labuan. there are two food and beverage outlets, a lobby lounge and in-room dining service is provided at the hotel. the feature comprehensive facilities, including a Business center and is committed to providing the best service. Be it for business or leisure, hotel aifa will ensure that your stay with us is an enjoyable one.
